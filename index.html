Privacy Policy

Your trust is important to us.

This Privacy Policy describes how Nagara Mitra ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application and services (collectively, the "Service").

Information We Collect

Camera Permission

We request access to your device's camera to enable QR code scanning. This feature allows you to quickly and easily book taxis without manual entry.

Location Data

We collect your precise location data when the app is in use to show you nearby available taxis, calculate trip fares, and provide real-time navigation to your driver.

Phone Number

Your phone number is required for account creation and security, primarily for One-Time Password (OTP) verification. It also allows your driver to contact you regarding your trip.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to operate and improve our Service. This includes facilitating taxi bookings, matching you with the nearest available drivers, processing payments, and ensuring safe and reliable transportation.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your information is only shared under the following limited circumstances:

  • With your assigned driver to facilitate pickup and communication.
  • When required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Nagara Mitra, our users, or the public.
